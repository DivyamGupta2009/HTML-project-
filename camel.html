Note

These instructions will show you how to make an origami camel. If you're a beginner, I recommend reading the beginner's guide first.

Step 1) Fold the paper in half.

Step 2) Fold the paper in half again.

Step 3) Open the top flap of paper over to the right.

Step 4) Squash Fold this flap down.

Step 5) Turn the paper over.

Step 6) Open the top flap of paper to the left and Squash Fold it down.

Step 7) Fold and Unfold the sides along the dotted lines.

Step 8) Open up the top layer of paper and fold it up using the crease.

Step 9) Fold in both sides along their creases making a Petal Fold.

Step 10) Turn the paper over.

Step 11) Repeat steps 7-9 on this side.

Step 12) Fold sides to centre along the dotted lines. Repeat on back.

Step 13) Inside reverse fold up both sides of the paper.

Step 14) Reverse Fold the back part down to make the back legs.

Step 15) Fold the top flap of paper down and repeat on other side.

Step 16) Reverse Fold the tip down to make the head.

Step 17) Inside Reverse Fold the tip of the head.

Step 18) Fold the back of the leg inside the model along dotted line. Repeat on the other side.
